Welcome

St. Joseph's College of Engineering & Technology, Palai, established in the year 2002, affiliated to M.G University and Kerala Technological University is a pioneer institute for Technical education in the self-financing sector, approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The Dept. of Computer Science & Applications (MCA), has started in the year 2009, offering both Regular and Lateral MCA courses. We, both the staff and students, are on a continues strive to seek out and explore new ways to improve..

- About Us

Fenstra 2K18 is the 6th edition of the prestigious National Level Inter-Collegiate meet. Conducted by the association of MCA students SMASH of St Joseph's College Of Engineering and Technology Palai.

Rules

  • Maximum two members per team.
  • Any number of teams from a particular college.
  • All rounds will be based on C programming language.

  • Top five teams from the second round will be selected to the third and final round.
  • The two teams with the highest scores will be declared as winners.
  • First round will be C based aptitude, second round will be debugging and final will be programming test.
